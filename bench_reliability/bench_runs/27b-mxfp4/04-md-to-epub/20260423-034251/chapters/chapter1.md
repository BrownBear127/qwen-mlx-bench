# Chapter 1: The Beginning

In the beginning, there was nothing but a vast expanse of empty space. The universe was silent, waiting for something to spark the first light.

## A New Dawn

Then, something happened. A tiny fluctuation in the fabric of reality gave rise to the first particle. From that single point, energy cascaded outward, creating matter, radiation, and the building blocks of everything we know.

## The First Stars

Millions of years passed before the first stars ignited. These ancient beacons burned brightly, forging heavier elements in their cores and scattering them across the cosmos when they eventually exploded as supernovae.

The universe was no longer empty. It was alive with light, energy, and the promise of something greater yet to come.
