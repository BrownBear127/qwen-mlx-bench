# Chapter 1: The Beginning

The sun rose over the quiet village, casting long shadows across the cobblestone
streets. A gentle breeze carried the scent of fresh bread from the bakery on the
corner, where old Mr. Hartwell had been working since before dawn.

Maria stepped outside and stretched. Today was the day she had been waiting for —
the annual harvest festival. Stalls would line the main square, musicians would
play until midnight, and the whole town would come together as they had for
generations.

She walked briskly toward the town center, her boots clicking against the stones.
The morning mist was beginning to lift, revealing the colorful banners that had
been strung between the lampposts overnight. Everything was almost ready.
